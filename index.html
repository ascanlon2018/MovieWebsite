Review of the 2013 Film Adaptation of "The Great Gatsby"

Directed by Baz Luhrmann

This 2013 film adaptation of F. Scott Fitzgerald's mangun opus has been long anticipated;The Great Gatsby" follows the exploits of its eponymous character, Jay Gatsby. Taking place during the roaring twenties on the fictious island of West Egg, New York, it begins with aspiring author Nick Carraway's first encounter with the ellusive Gatsby. Jay enlists Nick's help in the task of winning the affections of Nick's cousin, Daisy, ensued with heartbreak and revenge
